Senior Data Scientist

We are looking for a Senior Data Scientist to build, evaluate, and deploy ML models.
You will work with cross-functional partners to define success metrics, run experiments,
and communicate findings to leadership.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement production-ready models
- Collaborate with engineers, product, and stakeholders
- Present insights and recommendations clearly
- Mentor junior team members

Requirements:
- Strong analytical thinking and attention to detail
- Ownership and reliability in project delivery
- Adaptability in ambiguous environments
- Curiosity and a continuous learning mindset
